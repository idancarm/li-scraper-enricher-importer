# Example ICP Configuration

## Target Personas
- VP/Director/Head of Sales, Marketing, or Revenue Operations
- C-level executives (CEO, CRO, CMO) at companies with 50-500 employees
- Sales/Marketing leaders at B2B SaaS companies

## Company Criteria
- B2B SaaS or technology companies
- 50-500 employees (mid-market)
- Based in US, UK, or EU

## Exclude
- Consultants, freelancers, agency owners
- Students, interns, job seekers
- Companies with fewer than 20 employees
- Non-profit organizations
